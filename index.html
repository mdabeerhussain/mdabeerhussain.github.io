Notre Dame Debating Club (NDDC) is called the Father of Debating Club of the country. The club started its journey in 1953. It celebrated its Golden Jubilee in 2003.

Notre Dame Debating Club
Estd. 1953

Notre Dame Science Club also known as NDSC, is the oldest science club in the country. It was established in 18 September 1955 by the ex-principal of the college Father Richard William Timm, CSC. Every year it organizes the biggest science fair in college level of the country.

Notre Dame Science Club
Estd. 1955

Notre Dame Outward Bound Adventure Club is abbreviated to 'OBAC'. The very name of the club explains that it tends to augment the physical, mental and ethical development as well as initiate the versatile ambitions of a person. This club was established on 11 October 1966 with inspirations from Fr. Richard William Timm, CSC

Notre Dame Outward Bound Adventure Club
Estd. 1966

Notre Dame Eco and Space Club was established on 28th October, 1992. It is the 1st Astronomical & Space based club in country. We arrange workshop, seminars, field trips and stargazing camps to promote study of Earth, Eco and Astronomy among the youth generation of Bangladesh.

Notre Dame Eco & Spae Club
Estd. 1992
